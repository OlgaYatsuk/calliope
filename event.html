A wide field of activities

Since the 1990s, Austria has had a network for Austrian foreign culture and cooperation in the fields of science and education in Ukraine. Today, the Austrian Cultural Forum Kyiv and the Austrian Cooperation Office Lviv form the core of this network.

Both institutions aim to actively participate in Ukrainian cultural and scientific life through Austrian contributions and to create encounters that will result in long-term Austrian-Ukrainian cooperation and networking

Our field of activity is diversified and ranges from preserving the common Austro-Ukrainian cultural heritage to the presentation of contemporary Austrian art and the discussion of relevant topics of the future. Moreover, we promote cultural and scientific exchange between our countries in the form of residencies and scholarships offered by various Austrian entities.
